For most of the terminology, our translation bases itself on the official PSP
localization of Persona. However, there are cases where our translation choices
differ from Atlus'. The changes made are listed below, so that players may still
cross-reference the info with pre-existing guides and wikis.

Our criteria for changing stuff is as follows:

- No gratuitous Japanese!
- Demons/Personas of Chinese origin use Chinese names.
- Mistranslations/mispellings from the PSP translation were fixed.
